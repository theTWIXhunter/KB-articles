Backups are an essential part of managing your Minecraft server, allowing you to save, download, and restore your world to protect your progress. With backups, you can take snapshots of your server and restore them whenever needed. Whether you're preparing for major changes, recovering from a crash, or just safeguarding your hard work, backups ensure your server and its world are always secure and ready to use.

### Taking a backup
It is suggested to regularly take a backup to insure the safety of your server or buy a premium plan to get access to automated backups.

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Choose a server within your server list.

3. You will be redirected to your server's [Console page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console). In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Press the "New Backup" button.

5. Give your backup a clear name so you can find it back later.

6. Wait for the backup to finish, this can take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

### Restoring a backup
You can restore your backup to rollback recent changes to your server.

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Choose a server within your server list.

3. You will be redirected to your server's [Console page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console). In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Find the backup you want to restore.

5. press the 3 dots and select "restore".

{: .warning}
> This action will overwrite all existing files and progress on the server. be careful because this action is not reversible!

6. wait for the backup to be fully restored, this might take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

### Downloading a backup
You can download your backup incase you want even more protection and off-site storage.

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Choose a server within your server list.

3. You will be redirected to your server's [Console page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console). In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Find the backup you want to download.

5. press the 3 dots and select "download".

6. wait for the backup to be fully downloaded, this might take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

7. You can extract the .tar.gz archive on your device just like you would with other archives (like .zip).

### Deleting backups

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Choose a server within your server list.

3. You will be redirected to your server's [Console page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console). In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Find the backup you want to delete.

5. press the 3 dots and select "**New Port**{: .red }".

{: .warning}
> Be careful because this action is not reversible!
