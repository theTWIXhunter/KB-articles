Backups are an essential part of managing your Minecraft server, allowing you to save, download, and restore your world to protect your progress. Backups allow you to take snapshots of your server and restore them whenever you need to. Whether you're preparing for major changes, recovering from a crash, or just want to protect your hard work, backups ensure that your server and its world are always safe and ready to go.

### Taking a Backup
It is recommended that you back up regularly to ensure the safety of your server, or purchase a premium plan for access to automated backups.

1. Login to the [Dashboard] (https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Select a server from your server list.

3. You will be redirected to the [Console Page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console)  of your server. In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" and then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Press the New Backup button.

5. Give your backup a unique name so you can find it later.

6. Wait for the backup to complete, this may take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

### Restoring a Backup
You can restore your backup to roll back recent changes to your server.

1. Log in to the [Dashboard] (https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Select a server from your server list.

3. You will be redirected to the [Console Page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console) of your server . In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" and then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Locate the backup you wish to restore.

5. Press the 3 dots and select "Restore".

{Warning.}
> This action will overwrite all existing files and progress on the server. be careful as this action is irreversible!

6. wait for the backup to be fully restored, this may take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

### Downloading a backup
You can download your backup if you want even more protection and offsite storage.

1. Login to the [Dashboard] (https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Select a server from your server list.

3. You will be redirected to the [Console Page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console)  of your server. In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" and then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Locate the backup you wish to download.

5. press the 3 dots and select "Download".

6. wait for the backup to download completely, this may take a few minutes depending on the size of your server.

7. You can extract the .tar.gz archive to your device just like any other archive (like .zip).

### Deleting Backups

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Select a server from your server list.

3. You will be redirected to the [Console Page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console) of your server. In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" and then navigate to [Backups](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/backups).

4. Locate the backup you wish to delete.

5. Press the 3 dots and select "**New Port**{: .red }".

{: .warning}
> Be careful, this action is irreversible!
